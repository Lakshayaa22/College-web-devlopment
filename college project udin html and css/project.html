About Us

KIT is one of the leading educational institutions, dedicated to excellence in teaching, research, and community development. We offer a range of programs to prepare our students for a bright future.KIT, being one of the best Engineering Colleges in Coimbatore, strives to make a mark in the field of engineering and technology globally. Set in a calm and congenial locale, the college stands nestled in a spacious campus set in a vast area of 5 lakhs sq.ft with five storied buildings. KIT with its magnificent ambience, modern high-tech facilities and universal recognition has been accredited by NAAC with ‘A’ Grade & NBA. The college offers 26 wide range of programmes including 12 U.G, 7 P.G & 7 Research Programmes.
